About Bertha's Deluxe Chocolates

Founded in 2007 with the belief that a candy should taste like a candy without the worry of it going straight to your gut! That should be left to feeling lively and satisfied, not sick or sluggish. After countless hours of work, Bertha's Deluxe Chocolates is proud to serve up an array of super-yummy chocolates prepared with premium quality, locally produced, organic ingredients. Our sweet treats aren't only healthy for you, they are healthy for Mother Earth! Please enjoy!

About Bertha

Bertha Harper, the owner and founder of Bertha's Deluxe Chocolates, has always had an interest in nutrition and sustainability. Becoming a vegetarian at 11, Bertha has spent more than half her life on the lookout for high quality products that fit her dietary restrictions and allergies. With a big sweet tooth and little to choose from, Bertha decided to take on the task of creating guilt-free chocolates! Now Bertha's Deluxe Chocolates has products all over San Francisco and is growing more and more every day.
